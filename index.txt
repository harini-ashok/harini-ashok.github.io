# about

About me

I'm a Software Developer from Phoenix, AZ, currently working in real-time kafka streaming applications. I enjoy finding simple, inventive ways to solve complex problems in a multitude of business domains, leveraging the power of data and efficiency of automation to develop creative E-2-E solutions.
My love for studying, analyzing, visualizing all things data got me into the tech world, which eventually translated into my passion for creating data-driven, highly automated applications. I dip my feet into different waters, and my recent work has been reflective of my interest in Cloud technologies, Kafka data streaming, CI/CD automation. 
I spent a semester in grad school working on web applications in the MEAN stack, making a starter trading application. 

As someone passionate about working with data, I have previously worked on text and image data, writing scripts that leverage Machine Learning algorithms to derive patters from data, and even generate new data from learnt patterns.

Apart from my work in tech, I enjoy learning nutrition science, the science of fasting and related topics. I write about topics relating to this on my blog in the other tab, feel free to check them out. I am also an avid yoga practitioner, hoping to one day take it professionally.
